In collaboration with Visit Copenhagen, welcome to Assistens Cemetery

— a website for those who wish to explore the final resting places of some of Denmark’s most influential cultural figures. Here, you can discover the stories and legacy of iconic names such as Hans Christian Andersen, Søren Kierkegaard and Dan Turèll, all buried at the historic Assistens Cemetery in Copenhagen.

Follow in the footsteps of iconic figures

A walk among Copenhagen’s cultural names

Explore Assistens Cemetery and discover the final resting places of some of Denmark’s most influential cultural icons. Walk through the historic paths of Nørrebro and experience the stories, legacy and impact of the writers, thinkers and artists who helped shape Copenhagen’s cultural identity.
